About the Tournament

A passion for exercise and physical games is a great blessing. The Sports/Dream Garden Club is grateful for this blessing and is committed to promoting it.

In this spirit, the Club is excited to announce an upcoming badminton tournament. While we had hoped to organize multiple tournaments throughout the year, the unpredictable weather conditions at the Open Air Badminton Court have made this challenging.

The Community Center, originally planned by the Society Administration, remains incomplete. However, with the generous support of our friends, we are optimistic that we will be able to construct an indoor badminton court soon, Insha'Allah.

Our Patron-in-Chief, Adil Sheikh Sahib, has always been an active participant in all social and recreational activities. We are hopeful that he will continue to support the development of our club.

We are also honored to have Mian Zaheer Ahmed, a long-standing club member who travels from Saudi Arabia each year to participate. With his professional expertise in organizing events in Saudi Arabia, he plays a crucial role in ensuring our tournament is a success. He will be arriving in the last week of this month.

According to this schedule, the Two-day Tournament will be held in the first week of November, on 7, 8 November, Insha'Allah.

Every member of our sports club knows that this event is about more than just badminton. It's a celebration with a wonderful atmosphere, delicious food, and great entertainment for everyone. Plus, spectators will have the chance to witness some truly competitive matches!

To keep the competition exciting, we ensure that players of similar skill levels are matched against each other. In high-stakes games, you can always expect the unexpected, with the possibility of a major upset at any moment. We encourage all participants to play with a positive attitude, focusing on sportsmanship and enjoying the thrill of the game, rather than worrying about winning or losing.
