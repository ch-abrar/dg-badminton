Sports/Dream Gardens Club

"Health is Wealth" - Join us in celebrating sports and wellness

About the Tournament

Being passionate about exercise or any physical game is a great blessing from Allah. Sports/Dream Garden Club is grateful for God's blessing and keeps trying to promote it.

In this regard, the Club is going to hold a badminton tournament. We wanted to organize three or four tournaments in a year, but it was not possible due to the severity and uncertainty of the weather in the Open Air Badminton Court.

The Community Center, which was supposed to be built by the Society Administration, has fallen into disrepair. But with the support of friends, we will try to build an indoor badminton court very soon, Insha'Allah.

Adil Sheikh Sahib, Patron-in-Chief of DGC/Sports Club, has always actively participated in all Social and Recreational Activities. It is hoped that he will also play his part in its development.

Mian Zaheer Ahmed, an old Club Member, comes from Saudi Arabia every year to participate in the tournament. Organizing various functions in Saudi Arabia is part of their professional expertise. Therefore, they play a big role in organizing our tournament in a good manner. He is coming in the last week of this month.

According to this schedule, the Two-day Tournament will be held in the first week of November, on November 8th (Saturday) and 9th (Sunday), Insha'Allah.
